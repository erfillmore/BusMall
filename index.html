Welcome to BusMall's Market Analysis App!

BusMall is a startup. We hope to offer the end consumer a catalog of assorted high-end goods, to browse on long bus journeys!

Since catelog's are expensive to print and distribute, we are running a text product, in which you, lucky tester, have the oppurtunity to guide what final products we put in the magazine. Here is how it works:

  • First, please look at the three images on your screen.
  • Chose the product in which you are most interested relative to the other products on your screen.
  • Click on the image.
  • Do this fifteen times.
  • At the end of go 15, the app will give you a table in which it notes what product was the most popular.
